Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of small hand-sized corn or wheat tortillas topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Taco seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook meat with taco seasoning according to package directions
  2. Chop lettuce, tomatoes, and other desired vegetables
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave
  4. Assemble tacos by adding meat to each tortilla
  5. Top with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes
  6. Add sour cream and salsa
  7. Serve immediately with lime wedges
