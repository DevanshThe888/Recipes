Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fresh fish (salmon, tuna)
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi

Steps

  1. Rinse and cook sushi rice according to package directions
  2. Mix cooked rice with vinegar, sugar, and salt
  3. Let rice cool to room temperature
  4. Prepare fillings by cutting fish and vegetables into thin strips
  5. Place nori sheet on bamboo mat
  6. Spread rice evenly over nori, leaving space at one end
  7. Add fillings in a line across the rice
  8. Roll tightly using the bamboo mat
  9. Cut roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife
  10. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger
